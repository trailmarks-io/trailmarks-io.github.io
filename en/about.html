About Trailmarks.io

The directory for personal hiking stones and their stories

Our Mission

Trailmarks.io is a platform for hikers who want to share and discover their personal stone collections. Every hiking stone tells a story - of special moments on the trail, of beautiful discovery locations, and of the people who collected them.

The Concept

Our directory enables hikers to discover personal hiking stones near them, add their own stones to the collection, and track the journeys of stones. Through photos, locations, and stories, a vibrant community of stone collectors emerges.

Platform Features

  • Discover Stones: Find personal hiking stones in your area
  • Expand Collection: Add your own special stones
  • Share Stories: Tell the stories behind your discoveries
  • Track Journeys: See where your stones have traveled

Project Status

The hiking stones directory of Trailmarks.io is currently in the development phase. We are working on a platform that enables hikers to share their personal stone collections, discover new stones, and track their journeys. Stay updated through our GitHub page.
