About Trailmarks.io

The vision behind the digital revolution of trail stones

Our Mission

Trailmarks.io emerges from a passion for hiking and respect for the centuries-old tradition of trail marking. We believe that technology should not replace the natural beauty of hiking, but rather enrich it.

The Idea

Trail stones and cairns have always been silent helpers on our paths. They tell stories, point the way, and connect hikers across time and space. With Trailmarks.io, we bring this timeless wisdom into the digital age.

Our Values

  • Respect Tradition: We honor the history of wayfinding
  • Foster Innovation: We use technology for better experiences
  • Strengthen Community: We connect hikers worldwide
  • Protect Nature: We promote sustainable hiking

Project Status

Trailmarks.io is currently in the development phase. We are working on a platform that combines traditional wayfinding with modern digital tools. Stay updated through our GitHub page.
