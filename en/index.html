The Story of Trail Stones

Since ancient times, humans have used stones as waypoints. These simple yet effective markers have safely guided generations of travelers through unknown territories.

Ancient Origins

Ancient Romans and Celts marked their paths with stone cairns to provide guidance for travelers.

Modern Markers
